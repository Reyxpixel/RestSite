FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Are your products certified gluten-free?

Yes, all our products are made in a dedicated gluten-free facility and are certified to contain less than 20ppm of gluten, meeting international gluten-free standards.

Do you deliver across India?

We currently deliver to major cities across India. Please contact us to check if we deliver to your location. We're constantly expanding our delivery network.

How long do your products stay fresh?

Our products are made fresh and typically have a shelf life of 3-7 days depending on the product. We recommend consuming them as soon as possible for the best taste and texture.

Can I place custom orders for events?

Yes, we accept custom orders for events, parties, and special occasions. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your requirements.
